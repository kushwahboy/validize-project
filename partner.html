Managed Service Providers -Empowering the IT Backbone

Managed Service Providers (MSPs) are the unsung heroes of the digital world, managing the intricate web of IT services for companies of all sizes. Here is how Validize revolutionizes their operations:

The Complexity of MSP Operations

With an average client base of over 100, each with potentially hundreds of users, keeping track of every interaction is a daunting task. Verifying identities, ensuring authorized requests, and unlocking devices for the right person is a constant challenge.

Validize steps in to streamline this process. By providing a secure and efficient means of identity validation, MSPs can guarantee that every interaction is secure, accurate, and legitimate.

Securing Every Interaction

Validize ensures that any request, be it for a password reset, file permissions, or network access, is authenticated.

Two-Step Verification

Technicians can now engage in a two-step process to validate users, establishing a direct link to the organization they represent.

Pre-Approved Status (Coming Soon)

With this upcoming feature, technicians can quickly ascertain if a user is authorized for a particular request. If not, they can send a non-disruptive push request.

Multi-Way Validation

Validize empowers both the technician and the client's representative to validate each other's identities, ensuring a secure interaction from both ends.

Revenue Generation for MSPs

MSPs can offer Validize as a product, boosting their client's IT security or as a tool for their client's own validation needs, thus enhancing their reputation.

Managing Multiple Companies with Ease

Validize's Admin portal offering administrative functions like user management, role assignment, group organization, and policy implementation

With Validize, MSPs can transcend the complexities of identity verification, ensuring every interaction is grounded in trust, security, and efficiency.

Seamlessly integrates with your daily life

Validize introduces a natural flow to your routines. No more lengthy processes or paperwork: We believe in swift, efficient solutions that still uphold the highest standards of identity verification. With Validize, you can confidently validate (Validize) individuals and organizations without disruption to your day.
