Features

With Validize, you can securely store various identities in one app. Whether it is your role at work, your association with an organization, or any other aspect of your identity, Validize keeps it safe.

When the need arises, simply provide a secure code generated by Validize. This code acts as a key to validate your identity. It is a process that ensures the person on the other end knows who they interacting.

Your secret code remains confidential throughout its journey of verification. With Validize, your data is protected at every step, leaving no room for compromise
